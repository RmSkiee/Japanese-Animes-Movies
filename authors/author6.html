Akira Toriyama

Famous Work: Dragon Ball

Akira Toriyama, born April 5, 1955, is a legendary Japanese manga artist and character designer. He is best known for Dragon Ball and Dr. Slump. His works revolutionized the shōnen genre and influenced many manga artists. Toriyama is admired for his dynamic art style and unforgettable characters.
